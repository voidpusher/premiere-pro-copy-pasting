# Installing Instant Paste

Welcome! Here's how to get Instant Paste running in Adobe Premiere Pro. It takes about 2 minutes.

## What you need
- Adobe Premiere Pro 2022 or newer (Windows)
- The `instant-paste.zxp` file you downloaded
- The email you used at purchase

## Step 1 — Get a free ZXP installer
A `.zxp` is the standard Premiere extension format. To install one, use any free installer:
- **ZXP/UXP Installer** (recommended) — https://aescripts.com/learn/zxp-installer/
- or **Anastasiy's Extension Manager** — https://install.anastasiy.com/

Download and open the installer app.

## Step 2 — Install the plugin
1. Drag **`instant-paste.zxp`** onto the installer window (or click **Install** and pick the file).
2. When it asks about an unsigned/self-signed certificate, choose **Install anyway** — that's expected for indie plugins.
3. Wait for the "Installed successfully" message.

## Step 3 — Open it in Premiere
1. **Fully close and reopen** Adobe Premiere Pro.
2. Open a project (the plugin needs a project open to work).
3. Go to **Window → Extensions → Instant Paste**.

## Step 4 — Sign in
1. The panel shows a sign-in screen. Enter the **email you purchased with**.
2. Check your inbox for a 6-digit code (and an "Activate in Premiere" link).
3. Enter the code, or click the link — the plugin unlocks and stays activated on this computer.

## Step 5 — Use it
1. Copy any image (screenshot, browser image, or image file).
2. Click **Paste Clipboard** in the panel.
3. It lands in your Project panel and on the timeline. That's it!
